Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our collaboration.

How long does a Pvt Ltd registration take?

Typically 10-15 working days, assuming all documents (PAN, Aadhaar, Proof of Address) are in order. We handle all MCA filings.

What are the benefits of Startup India Recognition?

Benefits include 80-IAC tax exemption for 3 years, self-certification under labor laws, and eligibility for the ₹10,000 Cr Fund of Funds.

Do you charge a success fee for funding?

Our fee structure is transparent. We charge a nominal consulting fee for document prep and a mutually agreed success fee upon fund disbursement.
